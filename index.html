Our Story

James formed Unicorns in 2021 when he realised there was no fully inclusive netball club for the LGBTQIA+ Community. Having been a member of London Frontrunners, and with friends in LGBTQIA+ clubs for other sports, James realised the importance and benefit of creating a safe and inclusive environment for these communities to play sport. James asked his team mates Clare and Louise to help him get it off the ground, and with the help of our friends at Knights Men's and Mixed Netball, we held our first friendly session open to all in October 2021. Since then our committee has grown to include Al, Vanessa, Calvin and Nikki, and we're building partnerships with other LGBTQIA+ organisations.

Our aim is to make netball more accessible to everyone, regardless of their gender identity, sexual orientation or skill level. So, whether you haven’t played since school, play regularly at social level, or just want to give netball a try, you’ve come to the right place. This group is an inclusive safe space for all members of the LGBTQ+ community, and our allies.

Join us on the Court

We're an ever growing club and would love for you to join us! The options are detailed below, but we want to make it clear that we always intend to be a not-for-profit venture that’s financially accessible to everyone. If you’re on a low income and want to be involved, talk to us.

We still aim to promote a beginner-friendly environment that’s relaxed and fun, but as we start to offer memberships, all participants will need to register with us and agree to the code of conduct and club rules. This is so we can protect you and meet the requirements of venues we book. See below our membership options and click the sign up button to go to our registration form.

**The first two sessions will always be free for newcomers**
