Unicorns Netball Tournament Info Page

Location and Start Time

Dont be late!

The tournament will be held at Kennington Park Tennis and Netball courts, London, SE11 4BE . The nearest station is Oval (not Kennington, as the name would suggest). Please arrive from 11.45am, and no later than 12.05pm. The first centre pass will be at 12.15pm.

Registration

Its a health and safety thing!

Please make sure all of your team members have registered with the Unicorns LGBTQIA+ Netball Club before they play. This is so we can protect you and meet the requirements of the venue

Rules & Code of Conduct

Be kind to each other!

Please make sure all your team members have read our code of conduct, and arrive with short nails, hair tied back and all jewellery removed or covered up. For any players who are new to the game, these are the rules of netball

Games Format

Make sure you're ready to start on time!

All games will have two umpires, and will consist of two 7 minute halves with a 1 minute break at half time. Each team will be given their own games schedule on the day - please make sure your players are ready to start play promptly at the start of each fixture.

Refreshments

Keep hydrated!

We will have a tuck shop, where you can buy water, bananas and energy drinks, and we accept card as well as cash!

Raffle

Good luck!

We are also running a raffle on the day, with some fab prizes, including tickets to events, an array of netball-related items, and a very special star prize, which will be unveiled on the day! Please encourage your teams to buy lots of tickets, so we can raise loads of dosh for the club, and also our partner charity for this event, The Outside Project. Tickets can be bought by cash or card.

Weather & What to Wear

Apply that sunscreen!

The forecast a week in advance says it’ll be 23 degrees and partly cloudy, but we all know how that can change, so keep an eye on it and prepare accordingly. The courts are very exposed with very little shade, so we recommend bringing hats for when you’re not playing, sunglasses, and factor 50 sun cream. During breaks, you can go into the shady areas of the park for some relief from the sun.

Post-tournament Social

Dont run off, we have prizes!

After the tournament (about 17:00), we’ll be heading over to The Cock Tavern (approximately a 10 minute away) to award the tournament prizes and have some drinks after an epic afternoon of netball. They don't serve food but have said we can order from UberEats/Deliveroo so we can refuel.

After Party

Lets keep this party going!

For those who wish to party on into the night, we also have a space reserved at Zodiac Bar and Club near Warren Street from 10pm. Zodiac are our club sponsor, so while it seems like a bit of a trek, we’d like to thank them for supporting us by giving them some custom.
