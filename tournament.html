Location and start Ttme

Dont be late!

The tournament will be held at Somers Town Community Sports Centre, 134 Chalton St, London NW1 1RX. Please arrive from 11.00am, and no later than 11:20am. Opening speech at 11:30am and first centre pass will be at 11:40am.

Health, safety and wellbeing

Stay safe out there!

Please make sure all your team members have read our code of conduct, and arrive with short nails, hair tied back and all jewellery removed or covered up. We also have a few first aiders on hand, and two first aid kits.

Game schedules

We'll try to stay on time!

All games will have two umpires, and will consist of two 8 minute halves with direct change over at half time. There will not be a specific lunch break so make sure you make the most of the time you have between games. Life is unpredictable and so, although we will try to stick to these timings, please listen out for announcments incase we are running late. Click on the image to enlarge it and see more clearly!

Refreshments

Stay hydrated!

We will have a tuck shop, where you can buy water, energy drinks, energy bars, crisps and other goodies. We accept card as well as cash! While we do have a tuck shop, we aren't serving 'proper' food on the courts. So, we recommend you bring a light lunch to have during the breaks to keep your energy levels up. We will bring bin bags for waste and recycling.

Raffle

Fab prizes up for grabs!

We are also running a raffle on the day, with some fab prizes, including free memberships, gym passes and an array of netball-related items. Please encourage your teams to buy lots of tickets, so we can raise loads of dosh for our partner charity for this event, London LGBTQ+ Community Centre. Tickets can be bought by cash or card.

Weather and what to wear

Apply that sunscreen!

We expect that the weather to be hot so do check the forecast and prepare accordingly.
The courts are very exposed with little shade, so we recommend hats for when you’re not playing, as well as sunglasses and sun cream.
As this is a pride tournament please dress in your most fabulous, colourful queer kit! (Anything goes as long as it's safe to play in!)

Post-tournament social

Dont run off, we have prizes!

After the tournament (about 6pm), we’ll be heading over to Zodiac Bar and Club (near Warren Street) to award the tournament prizes and have some drinks after an epic afternoon of netball. They don't serve food but they have said we can order from UberEats/Deliveroo so we can refuel. Zodiac is our club sponsor, and are a fab queer space for everyone in the LGBTQIA+ community, plus allies - just like us! We love them because of how truly welcoming they are to everyone, and their themed nights are next level.
